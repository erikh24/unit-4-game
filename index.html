Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random Target Score at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to the target score. You lose the game if your score goes above the target score.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you. Once you click a crystal, you can figure out the value of it by comparing your new score to your old score.

Each time you start a new game, the values of the crystals will change.

Good luck!!
